Executive Analysis

The data reveals a precipitous decline in adoption readiness as professional tenure increases. While the "Digital Native" cohort exhibits a nearly frictionless interface with new IT protocols, the "Late-Career" demographic shows a resistance coefficient that is nearly 5x higher.

Strategic interventions must prioritize bridge-building programs that translate native digital intuition into established mid-to-late career workflows.
