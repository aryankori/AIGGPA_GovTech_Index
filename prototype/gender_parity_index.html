Gender Parity Index (GPI)

A metric measuring the relative access to education and economic participation of males and females, serving as a critical indicator for institutional stability and socioeconomic development.

man Male Input
woman Female Input
GPI = Female Adoption Rate / Male Adoption Rate

Fundamental Institutional Ratio

GPI = 1.0
Perfect Parity

check_circle

Total gender balance achieved. Equitable distribution of resources and technology adoption across both segments.

GPI < 1.0
Female Under-Adoption

warning

Significant gender gap favoring males. Indicates potential barriers to access or cultural friction for female users.

GPI > 1.0
Male Under-Adoption

warning

Inverse gender gap favoring females. Requires strategic analysis of male segment disengagement or niche targeting.
