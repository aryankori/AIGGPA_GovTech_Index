Architecture Specifications

The **Infrastructure Layer** serves as the computational bedrock, providing elastic scaling through the IndiaAI Compute Portal. It leverages National Informatics Centre (NIC) assets to ensure data residency and sovereign control.

Above this, the **Data Layer** unifies disparate government databases through secure APIs and the eSankhyiki metadata clearinghouse. The **Model Layer** democratizes access to state-of-the-art LLMs, while the **Application Layer** delivers citizen-centric AI services across the governance spectrum.
