Executive Analysis

The Revenue department leads digital adoption projections, primarily driven by integrated fiscal systems and high-frequency transactional data. Conversely, the Education board remains in a nascent phase, constrained by decentralized reporting structures and manual archival systems.

This preliminary ledger serves as the architectural foundation for the upcoming 2025 Comprehensive Data Harvest. By establishing these benchmarks, AIGGPA identifies priority zones for GovTech investment and policy intervention.
