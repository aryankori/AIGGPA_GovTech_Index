The Three-Tier Adoption Model

Every government employee is classified into one of three tiers

TIER 1

Active Adopters

सक्रिय उपयोगकर्ता

Uses IT tools daily; e-Office, e-HRMS, iGOT integrated into workflow

Intervention: Optimise & Retain
—
TIER 2

Hesitant Users

संकोची उपयोगकर्ता

Aware of tools, possibly trained, but defaults to paper/manual

Intervention: Motivate + Remove Barriers
—
TIER 3

Unaware Non-Users

अनजान गैर-उपयोगकर्ता

No exposure, no training, no access — "Does not even know"

Intervention: Awareness + Training + Hardware

Perceptions & Attitudes / धारणा एवं दृष्टिकोण

Rate each statement on a scale of 1 (Strongly Disagree) to 5 (Strongly Agree)

"I feel confident using e-Office for my daily work"

"I find government IT tools difficult to use"

मुझे सरकारी आईटी उपकरण कठिन लगते हैं

"I would use IT tools more if trained properly"

"My supervisor encourages digital tool usage"
